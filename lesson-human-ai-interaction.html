🧠 What is Human-AI Interaction?

Human-AI interaction is a back-and-forth conversation where the human gives direction and judgment, and the AI provides knowledge and execution — together creating things neither could make alone.

Metaphor: Think of an architect and a master builder. The architect (you) decides what to build and why. The builder (AI) knows every technique to construct it. Neither is enough alone — the architect can't build, the builder doesn't know what to build. Together, they finish in a day what would take a year solo.
